How We Work

Our 7-Step
Development Process

Every project follows a rigorous methodology — from requirement analysis to post-launch maintenance.

1
Planning

We define the full scope — identifying requirements, estimating resources, timeline, and cost. A clear scope document is produced before any code is written.

2
Systems Analysis & Requirements

We conduct thorough business analysis — interviewing stakeholders, mapping workflows, and documenting requirements so the solution perfectly fits end-user needs.

3
Systems Design

We architect the database schema, UI/UX wireframes, and module structure. Design documents are reviewed with the client before development begins.

4
Development

Our certified Oracle APEX and PHP/MySQL developers build module by module with regular live demos shared with the client at each sprint.

5
Integration & Testing

QA conducts functional, performance, and security testing. All modules are integrated and tested against requirements to ensure zero defects at go-live.

6
Implementation & Deployment

We deploy to Oracle Cloud or on-premise, migrate data, configure access, and conduct staff training to ensure smooth adoption from day one.

7
Operations & Maintenance

Post-launch support — bug fixes, performance tuning, new features, and upgrades. Our clients rely on us for years, not just the initial build.
